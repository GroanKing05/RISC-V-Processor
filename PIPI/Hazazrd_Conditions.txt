Conditions:

1 Instruction Combinations:

sd x1, 0(x2)
Any Instruction
Solution - Not a hazard

beq x1, x2, 0x8
If True, then it is a control hazard and 2 wrong instructions would alredy be present in the pipeline
Solution - Flush the pipeline
